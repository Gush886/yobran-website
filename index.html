About Us

Yobran Investment Ltd is a trusted and certified supplier of a wide variety of goods and services across Kenya. We are committed to excellence, timely delivery, and building lasting relationships. Our operations are guided by integrity, professionalism, and innovation — ensuring we meet diverse client needs with flexibility and transparency.

Our company is AGPO-certified under the Women category (Certificate No. NT/PPD/2023/DGW/14785) and registered with KRA (PIN: P051713899Y). We serve both government and private sector institutions and are passionate about empowering local communities through business.

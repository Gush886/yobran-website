About Us

Yobran Investment Ltd is a certified and AGPO-registered company dedicated to providing general supplies and business support services across Kenya. With our dynamic team and reliable delivery systems, we support both public and private institutions through ethical, cost-effective, and efficient service delivery.

We pride ourselves on fostering long-term partnerships with government agencies, SMEs, NGOs, and corporates by tailoring solutions to meet emerging needs in a fast-changing procurement landscape.

About Us

Yobran Investment Ltd is a certified, AGPO-registered Kenyan company offering multi-sectoral supply and support solutions. We specialize in bridging procurement gaps for both government and private clients with quality, efficiency, and integrity. Our core values are professionalism, reliability, and a deep commitment to sustainable impact through business.

Registered under AGPO (Women category) with KRA PIN P051713899Y, we take pride in serving government departments, schools, hospitals, NGOs, SMEs, and development partners across Kenya. Our strength lies in our diversity of products, streamlined sourcing, and unmatched customer service.

Our Services by Industry

Office & Administration

We supply comprehensive office materials including stationery (papers, files, pens, toners) and ergonomic office furniture like desks, chairs, filing units, and cabinets. We also support procurement of consumables, ID badges, registers, and custom branded items for everyday administrative operations.

Technology & ICT

Yobran provides complete ICT solutions including laptops, desktops, printers, networking hardware, projectors, and accessories. We also offer installation, software licensing, basic troubleshooting, and server hardware solutions for organizations upgrading or setting up IT infrastructure.

Construction, Electrical & Plumbing

We offer construction materials like cement, nails, steel rods, paint, safety boots, and gloves. In electricals, we supply industrial and domestic solutions including copper and aluminum cables, fluorescent tubes, LED bulbs, floodlights, switchgear, sockets, changeover switches, panel boards, conduits, and accessories for installations and maintenance. We also support solar lighting setups and backup power systems. Plumbing support includes water storage tanks, drainage pipes, manhole covers, pressure pumps, faucets, angle valves, ball valves, and installation fittings.

Branding & Events

We provide end-to-end corporate branding—design, print, and supply of banners, signage, business cards, uniforms, and marketing materials. For events, we offer tents, décor, PA systems, chairs, backdrops, stage lighting, and event-day support staff where needed.

Hospitality & Cleaning

We supply linens, cutlery, uniforms, aprons, and cleaning consumables for hotels, hospitals, and institutions. Our catalog includes mops, bins, brooms, detergents, gloves, air fresheners, PPE kits, and handwashing stations for full hygiene solutions.

Agriculture & Education

We support farmers with certified seeds, farm tools, sprayers, fertilizers, and irrigation kits. In education, we provide textbooks, exercise books, classroom furniture, lab kits, learning models, and teaching aids to schools, colleges, and NGOs.

Logistics & Procurement Support

We coordinate secure delivery across counties through trusted logistics partners and in-house arrangements. We also assist with custom sourcing, bid support, LPO fulfillment, procurement planning, and framework contracts where needed.

Our Trusted Partners

We proudly collaborate with both National Government agencies and Nairobi County Government departments to deliver high-quality goods and services that meet their diverse operational needs.

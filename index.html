About Us

Yobran Investment Limited is a Kenyan registered company committed to delivering high-quality goods and services to both public and private sector clients. We pride ourselves on reliability, professionalism, and a future-focused mindset that ensures we evolve with the changing needs of our partners. With diverse capabilities across sectors, we’re positioned to handle supply contracts of varying sizes and complexities — from everyday office and cleaning supplies to specialized equipment, consultancy support, and logistics coordination.
